Japchae

A beautiful Korean dish full of colors and textures. Light, flavorful, and a reminder of how food connects cultures. This is the international dish I want to try the most.

How to do the magic 🪄

Ingredients

  • Glass noodles
  • Carrots, spinach, mushrooms
  • Soy sauce
  • Sesame oil
  • Garlic

Recipe

  • Boil glass noodles until soft.
  • Stir fry vegetables in oil.
  • Add garlic and soy sauce.
  • Mix noodles with vegetables.
  • Add sesame oil for flavor.
  • Toss well and serve.

Preparation Time: 25 minutes

Difficulty: Medium

Be careful: Do not overcook noodles.
