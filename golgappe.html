Golgappe / Pani Puri

My all-time favorite — messy, chaotic, and absolutely perfect. Every bite is a burst of spice, tang, and happiness. It is said that we always eat pani puri with the people very close to our heart. Golgappe has always been my favourite since childhood and it's always a mood changer for me.

How to do the magic 🪄

Ingredients

  • 20 puris
  • Boiled potatoes
  • Chickpeas
  • Spicy mint water
  • Tamarind chutney
  • Chaat masala

Recipe

  • Mash potatoes and mix with chickpeas and spices.
  • Prepare spicy mint water and tamarind chutney.
  • Crack the top of each puri carefully.
  • Fill with potato mixture.
  • Dip into spicy water.
  • Serve immediately and enjoy quickly.

Preparation Time: 20 minutes

Difficulty: Easy

Be careful: Eat immediately before puris get soggy.
